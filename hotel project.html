Our Journey

The Story Behind Hatsey Kaleb Hotel

Hatsey Kaleb Hotel is a family-owned establishment founded with a simple mission: to create a space where travelers feel at home while experiencing the warmth and culture of Tigray, Ethiopia. With 20 comfortable rooms, a restaurant serving local delicacies, and versatile conference facilities, we offer more than just a place to stay; we offer a place where you belong. We blend tradition with modern comfort, providing exceptional service, a welcoming atmosphere, and personalized experiences for every guest. Whether for business, a cultural experience, or a peaceful getaway, Hatsey Kaleb Hotel promises a memorable stay. Welcome to Hatsey Kaleb Hotel—where comfort, culture, and hospitality come together.
Hatsey Kaleb Hotel is dedicated to providing a welcoming and professional experience for guests. We specialize in hosting meetings, congresses, and events while ensuring comfort, security, and quality service. Our goal is to create a seamless stay with hospitality that reflects the warmth of Tigray.

Why Choose Hatsey Kaleb Hotel?

At Hatsey Kaleb Hotel, we offer more than just a place to stay—we provide a welcoming and comfortable experience for business travelers, event organizers, and leisure guests. With modern amenities, spacious meeting halls, high-speed WiFi, and dedicated service, we make every stay seamless and productive. Whether you’re here for a conference, a relaxing retreat, or to explore the rich culture of Tigray, our serene environment, delicious cuisine, and warm Ethiopian hospitality ensure a memorable visit. Your comfort and security are our top priorities, making Hatsey Kaleb Hotel the perfect choice for a truly enjoyable stay.
